Opinions

These are some opinions that I currently hold. They are subject to change. I have expanded on some of these ideas in blog posts.

  • Software Engineering is not engineering
  • Computer Science is not a science (the German word "informatica" is far better)
  • End-users are not stupid
  • Software can be "complete", within a reasonable definition of complete
  • Memory is cheap, but not free
  • Intractable does not imply impossible
