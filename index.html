Humans Write to Think

Here is a collection of semi-long-form pieces of writing that I have used to process some of my own thoughts. These are not written from any point of authority, and could be fundamentally flawed. It is still worth writing down your opinions if they might be hypocritical or ignorant, since the act of writing formalises thinking. Weak opinions do not survive the process of writing. The beliefs and opinions that remain can hopefully be judged objectively as independent of one's own mind.
